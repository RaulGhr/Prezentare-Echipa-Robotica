Xeo Academy

OUR STORY

Teamwork, cooperation, discovery and fun are the words that perfectly describe the Xeo Academy team. In 2018 we wanted to share the spirit of FIRST and we chose to enter the FIRST LEGO LEAGUE competition. Our journey began at the first edition of the XeoTalks event. Seeing the teams that participated in XeoTalks, how dedicated they are to their work and the past of the Xeo team, we decided it was time to stand out. In the following days, we created the team logo, we chose the team name, we arranged the laboratory and in a short time we mounted the kit for the contest and the game table. The competition is spilt into three categories: fundamental values, research project and robotics. The robotics category has got something special, though, because the robot must be made out of only lego pieces. Since the team formed and by now, we have evolved a lot. We really put a lot of work in solving the missions, gathering information for reserch project and finding solutions for the problems that came in their way. We became friends with our older friends very quickly and we always spent time in each others labs. With each start of new school year, the team changes, but those from the last season will help unconditionally those who are at the beginning of the road.

Nationala 2019

26.04.2019

‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎ We were scared, curious, full of emotions but also extremely happy because we were participating in our first national stage of the FLL contest. For 6 months we prepared everything for this contest. The robot was ready, the research project was written and presented in physical form through project. In the evening before the competition, we prepared lists of everything we had to take with us and in the morning we got on the bus and left for Bucharest with the greatest emotions. On the first night at the hotel, the girls prepared small gifts for each team, some nets with pens, badges and 3D modeled keychains with the team logo. The morning of the contest was hard, but we managed to set up our stand and share the gifts. We had as a mascot a rocket made by us, bigger than us, the theme being Into Orbit. All day long we were at the match, we were shouting the name of the team with all our might and we were living some incredible moments, we even had to go to the jury for slavery, project and fundamental values. We were very successful in all of them, being appreciated for the joy and unity we showed. When it came time for the premiere, we were all sitting in a circle and we were all holding hands with our mentors who were always by our side and supported us at every step. We were sitting, waiting impatiently, and we hear: The award for the most innovative project is given to the team .... Xeo Academy from Alba Iulia. We were all happy running on the podium and we all raised the cup shouting XEO XEO XEO leaving the podium.

Proiect copii

26.04.2019

‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎ Four days before the regional competition in Cluj, the children from the “Apulum” Technical College, from the 4th grade, with whom we have a collaboration, managed to surprise us a lot. The children announced their unexpected visit 20 minutes before, in order to be as unnoticed as possible. When the children arrived, we arranged things in the laboratory and thought about what they could play with the children or what else to show them.

‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎ Suddenly we see the children walking through the laboratory door. They had a rolled sheet in their hand. We wanted to invite the children to the lab, but the children said we all had to leave because they had a surprise. I had no idea what the surprise might be. They took the sheet and opened it. When they opened it, we saw their logo drawn, some robots representing the team members, each drawing in a special way, many hands that were theirs and big "Good luck!" written on it. The moment was overwhelming for us, most of us started to cry and we formed a huge hug, after the group photo.

Copacul Valorior Fundamentale

26.04.2019

‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎ "Communication is the key." We also rely on this in the Xeo Academy team, preserving the fundamental values ​​of the FIRST competition. Communicating with other teams, both from the country and from abroad, made us understand how important this is and that without it we would not be able to get that far.

‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎ So, we thought of doing an international project in which to involve as many FLL teams as possible. We decided to make a 3D tree whose leaves and branches to put the fundamental values ​​of the contest, along with representative pictures of as many teams as possible. We designed a message in which we explained what we want to do and sent it, receiving positive responses in a very short time, along with their representative pictures. We built two trees out of cardboard that we cut in half and joined them, obtaining a 3D tree. We pasted the pictures of the 24 teams that contacted us, along with the picture of our team.

‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‏‏‎ ‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎‎ We united all the teams into one, showing the fact that, although we are rivals during the competition, nothing prevents us from being friends, because we all have the same goal: we evolve and have fun together.

Xeo ALUMNI

OUR STORY

Xeo ALUMNI represents all graduates of the Xeo robotics team in Alba Iulia who have crossed the threshold of the university. As part of such a beautiful community, although students, they continued to be part of the Xeo family, being present in all the activities of the Xeo and Xeo Academy teams. As trusted volunteers in regional and national competitions organized by Natie prin Educatie, they continued to proudly represent the name Xeo. As time runs by, Xeo ALUMNI began to host more and more graduates, and that can only enchant us because it means that Xeo, as a family, is growing stronger and stronger with each year that comes along.
