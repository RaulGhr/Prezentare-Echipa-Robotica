Last Seasons

Season on going

The theme of this year is Game Changers, a suiting name for difficulties that we are hoping to overcome alongside the other contestants. On this occasion, we have two options, the remote participation or the live one. Some things never change, like our iconic mentality, coined by Dr. Woodie Flowers, "Gracious Professionalism" being one of these exceptions. This motto represents fair play and an empowering spirit of competition. The challenges are as demanding as any other season, but this time we may participate in the remote events due to the coronavirus crises dissolving the alliances. But no matter what life throws at us we will always get right back up, and this competition is the starting point of showing it to everyone out there, as Mr. Flowers would have wanted.
