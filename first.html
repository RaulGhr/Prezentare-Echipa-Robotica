"Nație prin educatie" is an association of an impressive group of people who are making a change in Romania. They empower high schoolers to learn and make use of practical studies, like robotics, programming, marketing, and public relations, preparing them for the future through a get-up, try, and have fun doing it attitude that enables the youth to have contact with the real world. Up until now, their impact has influenced 77 cities, supporting each team to improve. Around 200 groups are competing and studying together, successfully causing a change in the educational system. The competition has a high difficulty level, thankfully it allows the participation of ex-members named alumni, and many more, being incredibly inclusive to everybody willing to help. All of this happened due to the ingenious idea of Dana Războiu, the president of the organization. She changed so many lives through these events, getting closer and closer to her vision. Dana stated: "I have a dream – that one day, in Romania, education will be a top priority. Only then, our country will have a chance to really shine.". We are forever grateful for everything that Nație did for us, and we hope that they will never stop improving education in Romania.

First Values

FTC is not only a way to compete with each other it is a way to better ourselves through the values that it teaches us. It is a way to engage the kids in kindergarten through high school in exciting ways of studying robotics and programming. To discover innovative ideas and develop skills accordingly, that we can use practically in the future, together, with all our differences aside, forming a family of teams. The lesson that shows how much we grow in this contest is balancing the teamwork under pressure with its other face of fun and unforgettable times. This entire idea came to reality due to the vision of Dean Kamen, who said that the objective of this competition is "to transform our culture by creating a world where science and technology are celebrated and where young people dream of becoming science and technology leaders." which gave us this great opportunity.
